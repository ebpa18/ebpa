Pharmacovigilance Certificate Program

Master drug safety monitoring, adverse event reporting, signal detection, and regulatory compliance.

12 Weeks Intermediate Offline Certificate

What You'll Learn

  • Principles of pharmacovigilance and risk management
  • Adverse Event (AE) and Serious Adverse Event (SAE) reporting
  • Case processing workflow and MedDRA coding
  • Signal detection and benefit–risk evaluation
  • Regulatory frameworks: ICH, CIOMS, CDSCO, EMA, FDA
  • Safety in clinical trials and post-marketing surveillance

Syllabus Outline

Module 1: Foundations

  • History, terminology, lifecycle
  • Global PV systems

Module 2: Case Intake & Processing

  • Sources, triaging, follow-up
  • Coding, narratives, causality

Module 3: Signal Management

  • Detection methods and tools
  • Assessment and validation

Module 4: Regulatory & Compliance

  • PBRER/PSUR, DSUR, RMP
  • Inspections and audits

Who Should Enroll

Pharmacists, life science graduates, clinical research professionals, drug safety associates, and anyone seeking a career in pharmacovigilance.
